# Sentinel Daily Audit Report — 2026-03-30

## Executive Summary

The system processed 1,904 articles over ~48 hours with 249 (13.1%) passing the keyword filter. **A critical keyword gap was identified: the standalone English word "drone" is missing from the keyword list**, causing 10+ articles about drone incursions into Baltic states (Latvia, Estonia) and Finland to be missed entirely. Additionally, 5 classification false positives were found where Haiku incorrectly inferred Poland as the affected country from Google News query strings, generating unnecessary SMS alerts about non-existent Polish threats. Seven real events were detected (drone/airspace incidents) generating 7 SMS alerts, though several were false positives for Poland.
