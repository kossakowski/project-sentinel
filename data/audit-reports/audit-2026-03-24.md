# Sentinel Daily Audit Report -- 2026-03-24

## Executive Summary

The system processed 1,939 articles across 30 active sources in 24 hours during a period of intense military activity (massive Russian strikes on Ukraine, drone incidents in Lithuania, Poland scrambling jets multiple times). The keyword filter has **two critical gaps**: (1) the Polish keyword `"drony"` fails to match singular/declined forms like "dron", "dronem", "dronow", causing ~6 articles about a **drone crash in Lithuania** (a target country) to be missed entirely, and (2) the absence of `"poderwał"` (scrambled) caused ~15 articles about **Poland scrambling fighter jets** to be missed. Additionally, the classifier systematically over-scored events -- conflating "Poland activated defenses" with "Poland was attacked" -- resulting in **at least 5 false critical/high alerts** including SMS alerts triggered by misclassified Ukraine-only articles. Three of four configured Telegram channels produced zero articles, indicating a connection problem.
